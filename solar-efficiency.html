Solar Efficiency

Backy's solar panels are engineered to be highly efficient, ensuring that you can charge your devices on the go, even in low light conditions. Whether you're on a hike or in a city, Backy has you covered with solar energy wherever you are. Backy uses Polycrystalline to ensure the best of both efficiency, and longevity. However, If you wish to use a different solar-panel you are entitled to ask for a different option when ordering your Backy. Additionally, all solar-panels here are enhanced with Backy's secret sauce.

Solar Panel Efficiency Table

Panel Type Efficiency Charging Time
Monocrystalline 20% 4 hours
Polycrystalline 18% 5 hours
Thin-film 12% 6 hours

Monocrystalline

Monocrystalline panels are known for their high efficiency and longevity. Best for high-energy devices.

Polycrystalline

Polycrystalline panels are slightly less efficient but are more affordable and versatile for different environments.

Thin-film

Thin-film panels are flexible and lightweight, perfect for portable solutions and budget-conscious consumers.
